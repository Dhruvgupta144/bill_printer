How To Generate bills
In the home page, you find an option "Add New Bill". Click it, then fill in the details and click the print button to print the bill and the "send email" button if you want to send the bill details to the customer.


How To Add Customer In Database
In the home page, you find an option "View Database" Click it, then click "view customer database"After that, a button named "add customer" will display. Click it, then fill in the customer registration form and submit it. You can verify by searching the customer's name in the database.


How To delete Customer In Database
In the home page, you find an option "View Database" Click it, then click "view customer database" After that, a button named "delete customer" will display. Click it, then fill in the delete customer form and submit it. You can verify by searching the customer's name in the database.


How To Add Product In Database
In the home page, you find an option "View Database" Click it, then click "view product database" After that, a button named "add product" will display. Click it, then fill in the add product form and submit it. You can verify by searching the product's name in the database.


How To delete Product In Database
In the home page, you find an option "View Database" Click it, then click "view product database" After that, a button named "delete product" will display. Click it, then fill in the delete product form and submit it. You can verify by searching the product's name in the database.


How To view previous bills detail
In the home page, you find an option "Fetch Bill Details" Click it, then you can view all the bills. You can filter the details using the customer's name or date.
